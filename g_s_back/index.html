Guess Game

Let's play a game!

Welcome to our wonderful application, to start playing, I ask you to enter in the first field your minimum value from 1 to 199. In the second field from the bottom, enter a value from 2 to 200. In the third field, enter the number of attempts from 1 to 15, and click the button Start. After Two buttons and an input field appear. Enter in the input field your value from the maximum to the minimum value that you have selected and press the generate button. If you want to end the game, click the exit button.
